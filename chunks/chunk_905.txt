[Sahih Muslim 1/397-400] At the daytime of the tenth of Dhul-Hijjah on the Slaughtering Day (Yaum An-Nahr) The Prophet [pbuh] delivered another speech. That was at high time morning, while he was mounting a grey mule. ‘Ali conveyed his statements to the people, who were standing or sitting. [Abu Da'ud 1/270] He repeated some of the statements that he had previously said the day before.
