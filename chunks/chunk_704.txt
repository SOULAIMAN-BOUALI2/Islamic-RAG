Shortly afterward, he and the others, rinsed their mouths and performed the evening prayer without ablution [Sahih Al-Bukhari 2/603]; he did the same for the night prayer [Maghazi Al-Waqidi p.112]. The following morning, at sunrise, the Muslims encountered the Jews when they had come out about their jobs with their axes, spades and strings driving their cattle along. They began to shout in surprise: "Muhammad has come along with his force!" The Messenger of Allâh [pbuh] said: "Allâh is Great, Khaibar shall face destruction. Behold! When we descend in the city centre, it will be a bad day for those who have been warned (but have not taken heed). [Sahih Al-Bukhari 2/603, 604]" For encampment, the Prophet [pbuh] had chosen a certain plot of land he deemed suitable to serve as the headquarters of his army.
