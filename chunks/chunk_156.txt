Al-Akhnas bin Shuraique Ath-Thaqafi used to detract from the character of the Prophet [pbuh] in season and out of season. The Noble Qur’? , in direct reference to this man’s ignominious deeds, attached to him nine abominable traits: "And obey not everyone who swears much, — and is considered worthless, a slanderer, going about with calumnies, hinderer of the good, transgressor, sinful, cruel — after all that base-born (of illegitimate birth)." [Al-Qur'an 68:10-13] [Ibn Hisham 1/356] Abu Jahl’s arrogance and haughtiness blocked all avenues that could produce the least light of belief in his heart: "So he (the disbeliever) neither believed (in this Qur’? , in the Message of Muhammad [pbuh]) nor prayed!" [Al-Qur'an 75:31] He, moreover, wanted to debar the Prophet [pbuh] from the Noble Sanctuary. It happened once that the Prophet [pbuh] was praying within the precinct of the Sacred House, when Abu Jahl proceeded threateningly and uttering abusive language.
