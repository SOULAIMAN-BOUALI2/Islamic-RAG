Thenceforth, that year bore that appellation. His Marriage to Sawdah [R] in Shawwal, the tenth year of Prophethood The death of Khadijah left the Prophet [pbuh] lonely. The name of Sawdah was suggested to him for marriage which he accepted. This lady had suffered many hardships for the sake of Islam. She was an early convert to the Islamic Faith and it was by her persuasion that her husband had embraced Islam.
