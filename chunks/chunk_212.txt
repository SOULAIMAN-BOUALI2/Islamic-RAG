[Al-Qur'an 54:2] [Bukhari (in several chapters); Za'd Al-Ma'ad 2/46; Ibn Hisham 1/350] The Final Phase of the Diplomacy of Negotiation The Messenger of Allâh [pbuh] left his confinement and went on preaching his Faith as usual. Quraish, likewise, repealed the boycott but went on in their atrocities and oppression on the Muslims. Abu Talib, the octogenarian notable, was still keen on shielding his nephew but by that time, and on account of the series of tremendous events and continual pains, he began to develop certain fits of weakness. No sooner had he emerged victorious from the inhuman boycott, than he was caught in a persistent illness and physical enervation.
