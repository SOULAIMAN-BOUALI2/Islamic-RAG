Banu Haritha of Al-Aws and Banu Salama of Al-Khazraj were partially impressed by the hypocrite’s behaviour. Both of them were overwhelmed by confusion and they had almost started to withdraw, but Allâh’s Care saved them from that disgrace. About their incident Allâh says: "When two parties from among you were about to lose their heart, but Allâh was their Wali (Supporter and Protector). He reminded the hypocrites of their duty at this delicate and awkward condition, but in vain.
