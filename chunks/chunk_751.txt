Indeed Allâh has preferred you above us, and we certainly have been sinners.’" [Al-Qur'an 12:91] Abu Sufyan observed ‘Ali’s counsel, to which the Prophet quoted Allâh’s Words: "He said: ‘No reproach on you this day, may Allâh forgive you, and He is the Most Merciful of those who show mercy!’" [Al-Qur'an 12:92] Abu Sufyan recited some verses paying a generous tribute to the Prophet [pbuh] and professing Islam as his only religion. The Muslims then marched on in a state of fasting until they reached a place called Al-Qadeed where water was available. There they broke fast [Sahih Al-Bukhari 2/613] and resumed their movement towards Mar Az-Zahran. The Quraishites were quite unaware of the development of affairs, but the Prophet [pbuh] did not like to take them by surprise.
