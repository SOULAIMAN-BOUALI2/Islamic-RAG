[Ibn Hisham 1/440-442] Ka‘b replied: "We have heard your words, and now O Messenger of Allâh, it is for you to speak and take from us any pledge that you want regarding your Lord and yourself." It was a definite stance showing full determination, courage and deep faith to shoulder the daunting responsibility and bear its serious consequences. The Messenger of Allâh then preached the Faith, and the pledge was taken.
