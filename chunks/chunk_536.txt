[Musnad Imam Ahmad 3/424] After committing all the martyrs to earth, and after offering praise and supplication to Allâh, the Messenger of Allâh [pbuh] went back to Madinah. On his way back, matchless examples of love and devotion were revealed by the truthful women believers; in no way less great than the men’s heroic deeds in the fight.
