[Al-Qur'an 8:12] Allâh, the All-Mighty, also inspired another message to His Messenger, saying: "I will help you with a thousand of the angels each behind the other (following one another) in succession." [Al-Qur'an 8:9] The Prophet [pbuh], in his trellis, dozed off a little and then raised his head joyfully crying: "O Abu Bakr, glad tidings are there for you: Allâh’s victory has approached, by Allâh, I can see Gabriel on his mare in the thick of a sandstorm." He then jumped out crying: "Their multitude will be put to flight, and they will show their backs." [Al- Qur'an 54:45] At the instance of Gabriel, the Prophet [pbuh] took a handful of gravel, cast it at the enemy and said: "Confusion seize their faces!" As he flung the dust, a violent sandstorm blew like furnace blast into the eyes of the enemies. With respect to this, Allâh says: "And you (i.e. Muhammad [pbuh]) threw not when you did throw but Allâh threw." [Al-Qur'an 8:17] Only then did he give clear orders to launch a counter-attack.
