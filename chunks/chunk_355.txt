Should any disagreement arise between the signatories to this treaty, then Allâh, the All-High and His Messenger shall settle the dispute. 10. The signatories to this treaty shall boycott Quraish commercially; they shall also abstain from extending any support to them. 11. Each shall contribute to defending Madinah, in case of a foreign attack, in its respective area. 12.
