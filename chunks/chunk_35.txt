[Tareekh Ard Al-Qur'an 2/104- 106] Rulership in Pan-Arabia We have previously mentioned the Qahtanide and ‘Adnanide emigrations, and division of Arabia between these two tribes. Those tribes dwelling near Heerah were subordinate to the Arabian king of Heerah, while those dwelling in the Syrian semi- desert were under domain of the Arabian Ghassanide king, a sort of dependency that was in reality formal rather than actual.
